Increase sales and average order value with the SprayPay monthly payment marketing tool on the product page.

== Description ==

With SprayPay – Gespreid betalen, you let your customers pay in a maximum of 36 months. With this you address a new target group, which results in an increase in turnover. In addition, our payment method achieves an increase in the average order value. With this plug-in, a monthly amount is shown on the product page bases on 36 months.

Here are a few advantages:

* Customers immediately see the monthly amount for products above €250,-
* Maximum amount is adjustable with a default of €5000,-
* This effectively increases turnover and increases the average order amount.
* Applicable for simple, variable and grouped products.
* Installed in seconds.

After installation you will find the settings in the Woocommerce sidebar. Questions about the plug-in or its installation? Contact us via <a href="https://www.spraypay.nl/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">www.spraypay.nl</a>.

=== Minimum Requirements ===

* WooCommerce 5.9 or greater
* WordPress 5.4 or greater
* PHP version 7.2 or greater

=== Installation ===

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` folder.
1. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` screen in WordPress
1. Proceed with the plugin's settings in admin area under `WooCommerce -> SprayPay Marketing Tool` admin menu
